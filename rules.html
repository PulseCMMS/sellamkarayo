⚽ Football Bingo

Generate your unique Bingo card. During the match, tap the squares as the events happen. First one to get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!

🔮 Match Predictions

Submit your predictions before kickoff and lock them in. Points are awarded as follows:

  • • Winning Team: 5 pts
  • • Final Score: 10 pts
  • • First Goal Team: 4 pts
  • • First Goal Scorer: 8 pts
  • • Total Goals: 3 pts
  • • Yellow Cards: 3 pts
  • • Red Cards: 3 pts
  • • Corners: 2 pts
  • • Match Goes To Extra Time: 3 pts
  • • Penalty Shootout: 3 pts
  • • Man of the Match: 5 pts
